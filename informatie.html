WELCOME TO SEOUL SISTA

Seoul Sista is the new talk of the town in Rotterdam. At Korean barbecue you prepare your own meat and vegetables on a grill that is built into the table. We are an all-you-can-eat restaurant where you can dine for a fixed price. This allows you to enjoy all our dishes and discover the Korean cuisine.
If you come by car, you can easily park at our neighbors of the Q-park Weena.
We are open from 4:45pm to 11:00pm and operate 2 shifts. In the overview below you can see our prices.
